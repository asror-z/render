Exporting chart to image

Built-in export

amCharts 4 has image and data exporting functionality built-in. Just enabled export menu and you’re all set.

You can export charts to most popular image and vector formats like PNG, JPEG, or SVG, as well as PDF. Data can be exported as CSV, JSON, or XLSX.
